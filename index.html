Briscoe's Favourite Salads

  • Briscoe's "Bucket of Vegetables" Salad

    This is one of those salads that is basically just a big old bucket of vegetables that has been mixed together. It takes a long time to eat, but is quite simple to prepare and tastes OK. It gives the eater the impression that they are a farm animal.

  • Briscoe's "Big as my head" Salad

    This is one of those salads that is literally as big as your head! It is inspired by Peter Attiah and his comments on Fuji Apples and his normal lunch. It takes a long time to eat, but is quite simple to prepare and tastes OK. It gives the eater the impression that they are a farm animal.

  • Briscoe's "What, another salad?" Salad

    This is one of those salads that makes you think "another salad, huh? I wonder if it is actually any different from the last two...". It takes a long time to eat, but is quite simple to prepare and tastes OK. It gives the eater the impression that they are a farm animal.

  • Briscoe's "How many salads does this guy eat" Salad

    This is one of those salads that is leaves you wondering "WTF - if this guy only has recipes for salads, is that all he eats?". It takes a long time to eat, but is quite simple to prepare and tastes OK. It gives the eater the impression that they are a farm animal.
